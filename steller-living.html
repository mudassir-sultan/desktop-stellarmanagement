LIVE IN STELLAR COMFORT

Residents of Stellar properties experience first-hand the company’s dedication to providing its residents a home that fits their lifestyle, something much greater than just a place to live in. We believe that the improvements we make in our buildings, from modernized lobbies and elevators to upgraded appliances and renovated bathrooms, provide its residents with a higher quality of life. Stellar’s portfolio stretches across New York’s five boroughs and Westchester and features properties that range from the classic pre-war architecturally charming walk-up to modern glass-skinned high rises.

A dedicated, maintenance and management team supports each building to ensure your every request is addressed with reliable, attentive service. Regardless of time or date, our staff is available to ensure that your home never interferes with your life.

Stellar Management has a long history of improving residential neighborhoods through its committed social responsibility efforts. We are passionate about a cleaner environment and many of the enhancements made to our buildings reflect this green ambition. A sampling of our eco-friendly efforts include: 

  • Energy-efficient appliances 
  • Energy-saving fluorescent light bulbs
  • Low-VOC paints for interiors
  • Installation of innovative fog-catchers
  • Ongoing commitment to reducing carbon footprint

Remarkable apartments, environmental responsibility, and reliable. Our best in class service allow us to maintain a superior level of satisfaction among our residents. It’s what we refer to as Stellar Living, and we look forward to you experiencing it for yourself.
